About SmartCardLink vCard

SmartCardLink vCard is your digital business card — the smart and seamless way to instantly share your contact profile and more. Just tap your card using NFC or have someone scan your unique QR code, and your details appear instantly on their phone with no app or download needed. It is quick, modern, memorable, and built to help you make stronger connections with a premium first impression.

FAQ

How does SmartCardLink work?

You tap the card on a compatible phone or let someone scan the QR code, and your live digital profile opens instantly.

Do people need to install an app?

No. The experience is designed to work without requiring the other person to install an app.

Can I update my information later?

Yes. Your digital profile is built to remain useful when your details change.

What is the difference between Standard and PRO?

Both provide the core smart-sharing system. PRO adds custom theme color, resume controls, analytics, reminder support, and a stronger premium presentation.
