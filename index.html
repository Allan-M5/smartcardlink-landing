Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

SmartCardLink is simple and magical, just tap your card on any compatible smartphone, and your profile instantly pops up! Share your contact details, social links, and more with just one tap. No app is needed for the person you’re sharing with.

What phones are supported?

Most modern smartphones support SmartCardLink! Nearly all Androids and iPhones made since 2018 with NFC enabled can instantly read your card. If someone has an older device, you can always share your unique QR code by scanning with phone camera.

Can I update my details later?

Absolutely! Life changes, your info should too. Just share with us your updates and we do it for you ASAP. Your changes go live instantly, so your card always stays up-to-date.

Do I need to install an app?

Nope! Neither you nor the people you connect with need to download any app. Your SmartCardLink works seamlessly with built-in smartphone features.

Is there a monthly fee?

No monthly fees here! SmartCardLink requires a one-time purchase for your card. All standard features, including profile updates and QR sharing, are included forever. Only after 1st year of use you are required to pay yearly subscription fee at Ksh. 500.

Is my information secure?

Yes, your privacy matters! Only the details you choose to share are visible when someone taps your card, and you choose who to share your personal profile with.

How do I share with someone who doesn’t have NFC?

No worries! Each SmartCardLink comes with a QR code. Anyone can scan it using their phone’s camera to access your profile, no special technology or app required.

Can I use it for business and personal info?

Definitely! Set up your profile however you like, whether you want to network professionally, share personal details, or both.

What if I lose my SmartCardLink card?

Don't panic! Your profile and data are safely stored online. Simply log in and you can order a replacement card at Ksh 500, your connections continue without a hitch.
