Questions People Ask Before They Upgrade

How does SmartCardLink work?

You tap your card on a compatible smartphone or let someone scan your QR code, and your live digital profile opens instantly.

Do people need to install an app?

No. SmartCardLink is designed to open through phone-native NFC or QR access with no app required for the other person.

What can someone do after opening my profile?

They can call, WhatsApp, email, save your contact, visit your website or portfolio, view your social links, check directions, see your hours, and use other actions you choose to make available.

Can I update my information later?

Yes. Your SmartCardLink profile is built to stay useful as your role, contacts, links, and business details evolve.

Does SmartCardLink work for both business and personal use?

Yes. You can use it for professional networking, client-facing business presentation, or even a more personal smart profile depending on how you set it up.

What happens if someone’s phone does not use NFC?

No problem. Every SmartCardLink setup also works through QR scan, so access remains quick and reliable.

What makes PRO different from Standard?

PRO unlocks custom theme color, resume / CV section, secure resume access control, analytics, reminder tools, and a stronger premium presentation for employers and clients.

How does PRO resume security work?

PRO uses a 4-digit resume password to control who can view or download your resume section, giving you stronger control over professional sharing.

What does analytics access help with?

Analytics helps you understand engagement with your profile, which is useful when your card is being used for job seeking, sales, branding, or client follow-up.

What is the reminder feature on PRO?

The reminder tool helps keep important contacts and follow-up opportunities warm, adding more practical value beyond just sharing details.

Can PRO change theme color?

Yes. PRO supports luxury preset colors and custom theme color selection so your vCard can feel more branded and distinctive.
