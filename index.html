A Smarter Identity Than an Ordinary Business Card

SmartCardLink vCard turns one introduction into a full premium digital experience. Instead of handing over a paper card that can be lost, ignored, or forgotten, you give people instant access to your live profile through NFC tap or QR scan. Your profile can open direct call, WhatsApp, email, save-contact, booking, business website, portfolio links, Google Maps directions, working hours, and social media in one polished place. For professionals who want stronger credibility, PRO unlocks custom theme color, protected resume access, analytics insight, and reminder tools that make your networking feel more powerful and more intentional.

Questions People Ask

How do the NFC and QR features work?

You tap the card on a compatible phone or let someone scan the QR code and the live profile opens instantly.

Do people need an app to use SmartCardLink?

No. The experience is designed to work through native phone behavior, making access easier and faster.

Which phones can use it?

Modern smartphones can open the profile by NFC or QR, and QR gives reliable access even where NFC is not active.

What happens if NFC is not available?

QR keeps the same instant-access experience alive, so your profile is still easy to open and use.

Can I update my profile later?

Yes. SmartCardLink is meant to evolve with your role, business details, contacts, links, and growth.

What does Standard include?

Standard covers the premium live digital profile, QR + NFC sharing, contact actions, business details, social links, maps, and gold theme styling.

What does PRO add?

PRO adds stronger branded flexibility through theme color, resume / CV support, protected access, analytics, and reminder tools.

How does custom theme color work on PRO?

PRO is designed for a more premium custom look so your digital profile can feel more aligned with your personal or business identity.

How does resume access protection work?

PRO allows better control around professional document access, helping serious viewers get the right information more intentionally.

How do analytics help?

Analytics gives you a better sense of profile engagement, which is useful for job seeking, sales, and client-facing business use.

How do reminder tools help?

Reminders help you keep important people, follow-ups, and opportunities warm instead of losing momentum after first contact.

Can I add my business website and portfolio links?

Yes. SmartCardLink is built to support your strongest links so visitors can see more than just basic contact details.

Can maps and working hours be shown?

Yes. These details help customers and visitors act more easily, especially when location and timing matter.

Is booking supported?

Yes. The profile can be structured to support faster appointment or action-taking flow when required.

Can social links be added?

Yes. Social links are part of the broader digital profile experience and help round out your online presence.

Who is SmartCardLink best for?

It works well for professionals, founders, job seekers, business owners, creators, service providers, and anyone who wants a sharper first impression.

Why is it better than a paper card?

Because it is richer, easier to act on, less forgettable, and more useful long after the first meeting ends.
