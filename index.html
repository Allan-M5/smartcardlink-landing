FAQ

Things people usually ask first

How does SmartCardLink work?

You tap your card on a compatible phone or let someone scan your QR code. Your live profile opens instantly, making it easy to save your details or contact you right away.

Do people need to install an app?

No. The experience is designed to work without requiring the other person to install any app.

What phones can use it?

Most modern smartphones can use the QR option, and many newer phones also support NFC tap reading.

Can I update my information later?

Yes. Your digital profile is built to remain useful when details change.

What is the difference between Standard and Pro?

Both packages give you the core smart-sharing system. Pro is aimed at clients who want a stronger premium look and a more elevated branded impression.

Is there a monthly fee?

No monthly fee. Renewal is yearly after the first year at KSh 500.

What link should I use to apply?
